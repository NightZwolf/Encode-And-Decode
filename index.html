Welcome to The Encryption Tool

How To Encrypt

1. Type your message in the encoding section.

2. Enter the shift number by which you want to encrypt your message.

3. Acquire the encrypted text and send it to your friend with the shift number.

4. Navigate to the Ecryption Page from the top right corner of the screen.

How To Decrypt

1. Type the encrypted message that you want to decode.

2. Enter the shift number by which the message was initially encrypted.

3. Acquire the original text.

4. Navigate to the Decryption Page from the top right corner of the screen.
